PlayerEx Reference

Overview

PlayerEx is an extension to the OP2 _Player class. Among some of the features it adds are getting/setting the player name, color number, and maximum ore capacities; as well as low level access to view, modify, and cancel player commands.
PlayerEx is derived from _Player, so all _Player methods and variables are accessible from the PlayerEx class.
